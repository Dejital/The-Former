The Former

The ongoing five year effort of the Kansas City based band The Former, once known as The Roketa Project, culminating to the release of the debut album Fall From Grace. The group consisting of two members Mikhail Marchenko and Sergey Kutserubov worked closely on creating a soundscape that spans many genres. The variety of the music recorded offers a mix of electronic funk, dark alternative rock and reggae-pop melodies.
